Contact Info

My name is Saban Turgut Cilsal. I was born in Kayseri, Turkey in 1991. I am a Biomedical Engineering M.Sc. graduate with a Manufacturing (mechanical) Engineering B.Sc. background.

Summarizing my academic and professional experience, I have majored in Manufacturing Engineering at Istanbul Technical University (ITU) and graduated in the summer of 2014. During my studies in ITU, I have completed my internships at Arcelik A.S. and Hidromak A.S, in 2013 and 2012 summers, respectively.

After graduation, I have worked for Ford Motor Company between dates September-2014 and July-2016, as a Product Development Engineer in the Body Exterior Department.

I have completed Biomedical Engineering MSc in Biomechanics/Biomaterials track at Politecnico di Milano, in April-2020.

Starting from July-2021, I am a part of Italian National Research Council as a Research Fellow, working on projects mainly focused on 1) Programming of query based Decision Support Systems applications in Healthcare field, and 2) Development of an auxiliary device for collaborative robots that provides information on the status of the cobot.
